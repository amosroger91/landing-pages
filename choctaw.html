Our Foundation

A Full Spectrum of Care in a Secured Setting

The Choctaw Nation Nursing Home focuses on comprehensive care for the whole person, including skilled nursing, memory care, and rehabilitation. Our modern facility provides a homelike atmosphere where all utilities are included and residents enjoy peace of mind with our secured community.

What We Offer:

  • **Secured Community:** Dedicated memory care, long-term care, and respite services.
  • **Holistic Health Services:** Dental, Diabetic, Hearing, and Vision care, plus complimentary transportation.
  • **Engaging Lifestyle:** Scheduled Daily Activities, musical performances, and social outings.
