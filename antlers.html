Our Foundation

Comfort, Convenience, and Professionalism

Antlers Manor is an integral part of the Antlers community. We are conveniently located immediately across the street from **Pushmataha Hospital** and offer physician services available 24/7. We are dedicated to individualized treatment plans and restoring independence.

What We Offer:

  • **24/7 Specialized Nursing:** Wound Management, IV Therapy, and long-term trach care.
  • **Post-Hospital Rehabilitation:** Physical, Occupational, and Speech Therapies in our dedicated Rehab Gym.
  • **Long Term Care & Respite:** Traditional residency options and short-term respite stays.
